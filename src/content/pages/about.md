# About Olmeda Pet Studio

## Our Mission

We help pet businesses grow through proven marketing systems. No fluff. No guessing. Just results.

## Who We Are

Olmeda Pet Studio was founded on a simple insight: most pet businesses have amazing services but terrible marketing. They're invisible on Google, they don't get reviews, and they lose customers to competitors who do this stuff right.

We fix that.

Our founder, Amanda Almeida, spent 10 years in digital marketing before specializing in the pet industry. She realized that pet businesses need different strategies than tech companies or e-commerce. We built our entire system around pet business growth.
