# Google Positioning for Pet Businesses

Your pet business is great. But if customers can't find you on Google, they'll go to your competitors instead.

**Here's the problem:** 76% of customers searching for pet services click on the first 3 Google results. If you're not there, you're invisible.

**Our solution:** We take you from page 3 (or not found) to the first page of Google—consistently.

## How It Works

### Phase 1: Discovery & Audit (Weeks 1-2)

We analyze:
- Your current Google ranking for key terms
- Your competitors' strategies (what they're doing right)
- Your website optimization gaps
- Your Google Business Profile completeness
- Customer review velocity and sentiment

**Deliverable:** Competitive analysis report + opportunity roadmap

### Phase 2: Foundation Building (Weeks 3-6)

We optimize the fundamentals:
- **Google Business Profile:** Complete optimization with photos, services, and posts
- **Website SEO:** Title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, keyword targeting
- **Technical SEO:** Site speed, mobile optimization, schema markup
- **Content Strategy:** Identify high-opportunity keywords for your services

**Deliverable:** Optimized Google Business Profile + website updates

### Phase 3: Authority Building (Weeks 7-12)

We build Google's trust in your business:
- **Review Generation System:** Systematic approach to getting 10-15 new reviews monthly
- **Local Backlinks:** Partnerships with local vets, pet businesses, directories
- **Content Creation:** Monthly blog posts targeting local search terms
- **Social Proof:** Leverage customer testimonials and results

**Deliverable:** Review generation system + monthly blog posts + backlink partnerships

### Phase 4: Optimization & Scaling (Weeks 13-16)

We analyze what's working and double down:
- Monitor ranking improvements (target: top 3 for primary keywords)
- Adjust strategy based on performance data
- Expand to new keyword opportunities
- Train your team on ongoing maintenance

**Deliverable:** Monthly reporting + sustainability plan
